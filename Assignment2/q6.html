Homemade Pizza Recipe

  • Home
  • Recipes
  • Tips

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough
  • 200g tomato sauce
  • 150g mozzarella cheese
  • 50g pepperoni slices
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 220°C (425°F).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough onto a baking sheet.
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  4. Top with mozzarella cheese and pepperoni slices.
  5. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle oregano, salt, and pepper.
  6. Bake for 10-12 minutes, until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted.
  7. Slice and serve hot!

Nutritional Facts

Calories: 300 per slice

Protein: 15g | Carbs: 35g | Fat: 15g
